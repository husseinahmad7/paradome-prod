# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, technical hiring managers, collaborators, and clients visit the portfolio to understand Hussein Ahmad's engineering focus, proof of production experience, and contact paths quickly. Many arrive from LinkedIn, search, or a shared resume link and need a clear seniority signal within the first viewport.

## Product Purpose

The portfolio presents Hussein as a full-stack, DevOps, AI, and MLOps engineer with credible delivery across ERP platforms, AI search systems, and deployment workflows. Success means visitors immediately understand what Hussein builds, see evidence from real projects and roles, and can contact him or inspect live work without hunting.

## Brand Personality

Technical, direct, rigorous, and pragmatic. The page should feel like a working engineer's portfolio: confident and inspectable, with enough visual personality to be memorable but no decoration that hides the substance.

## Anti-references

Avoid toy-like sci-fi effects, clipped decorative hero elements, generic Bootstrap list pages, long stack dumps, weak gray text, hidden section headings under fixed navigation, and portfolio copy that reads like future intent when the resume shows production work.

## Design Principles

- Lead with role clarity and production proof.
- Make experience capability-led before naming tools.
- Keep motion useful, optional, and accessible.
- Make every section scannable on mobile first.
- Use visual identity to support credibility, not distract from it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible keyboard focus states, stable fixed navigation, reduced-motion support, descriptive image alt text, readable mobile line lengths, and no content that depends on JavaScript animation to become visible.
